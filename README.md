# Hey, I'm Stefan

ML engineer who likes to ship things. Currently finishing my MEng at [Cornell Tech](https://tech.cornell.edu/) and doing medical imaging research at Weill Cornell Medicine (Sabuncu Lab).

Previously built and deployed a clinical risk prediction model across 9 hospital wards at Iceland's National University Hospital. 15 years of patient data, 87% of the population, 5.3× risk stratification, SHAP explainability for every prediction.

Currently working on projects in LLM fine-tuning, agentic AI, and NLP. Repos coming soon.
